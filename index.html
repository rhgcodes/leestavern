story

We wanted to find a place where we can eat delicious meals, drink authentic beverages & have a lot of fun.

We haven’t found one, so we created it. While having a lot of fun.

So we called out crazy friends and started to think about nice ideas.

A lot of awesome things were mentioned and we focused on making the place as comfy & familiar as possible.

Socializing and meeting new people is on the first place.
